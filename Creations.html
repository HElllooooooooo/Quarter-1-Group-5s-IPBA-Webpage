HOW DO WE MAKE OUR SHADOW PUPPETS

MATERIALS IN CREATING OUR PUPPETS

  • Folder
  • Pencil
  • Eraser
  • Ballpen
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Coloring materials
  • Eye fastener
  • Stick
  • Puncher

HOW DO WE MAKE OUR PUPPETS

  1. Trace the puppet shape on a folder.
  2. Cut out the traced shape.
  3. Use a puncher to make small holes in the puppet.
  4. Apply acrylic paint or coloring materials to add color to the puppets.
  5. Attach eye fasteners to the holes.
  6. Use strings to attach sticks to the puppets.

CREATING SHADOW PUPPETS SHOW

Creating puppets not only sparks creativity but also helps improve artistic skills. It is an enjoyable way to entertain and inspire others, especially children. By sharing our puppet-making experiences, we can encourage others to explore their creativity and create their own unique puppets.
